# TestFlight Release Notes

## Version 1.0.0 (Build 1)

### What's New
First beta release of SliceFix AI - your AI-powered golf swing coach that identifies and fixes your slice.

### Features to Test
- **Onboarding Flow**: Swipe through the introduction screens
- **Authentication**: Sign in with Apple or Email OTP
- **Video Upload**: Upload a swing video from your library
- **Video Recording**: Record a new swing video
- **Sample Analysis**: Use the "Sample Video" option to test analysis without uploading
- **AI Analysis**: View your personalized swing analysis with root cause identification
- **Challenge Items**: Complete the recommended drills and corrections
- **Subscription**: Test the paywall and subscription flow (use Sandbox account)
- **Settings**: Test sign out, restore purchases, and account deletion

### Known Limitations
- Analysis requires network connection
- Videos are automatically deleted within 24 hours for privacy
- Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone

### Feedback
Please report any issues or feedback to: support@cienrios.com

## Test Scenarios

### Critical Paths
1. Complete onboarding and sign in
2. Upload a swing video and receive analysis
3. Review and complete challenge items
4. Subscribe to premium (use Sandbox)
5. Sign out and sign back in
6. Restore purchases

### Edge Cases
1. Try analysis with poor network connection
2. Cancel during video upload
3. Test with maximum video length (15 seconds)
4. Test account deletion flow
