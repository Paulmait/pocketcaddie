### Full Description (4000 chars max)
```
Stop slicing. Start scoring.

SliceFix AI analyzes your golf swing using advanced AI technology to identify the root cause of your slice and provides personalized drills to help you improve.

HOW IT WORKS
1. Record a 5-8 second swing video
2. Our AI analyzes your technique
3. Get your personalized root cause analysis
4. Follow targeted drills designed for your specific issue

KEY FEATURES

• AI-Powered Analysis
Upload your swing video and receive instant feedback on what's causing your slice. Our AI identifies common issues like open clubface, over-the-top swing path, and grip problems.

• Personalized Drill Recommendations
Get drills specifically matched to your root cause. Each drill includes step-by-step instructions and tips for maximum improvement.

• Progress Tracking
Track your improvement over time with visual charts and analysis history. See how your swing evolves and celebrate your progress.

• Before/After Comparison
Compare your baseline swing to recent swings and visualize your improvement journey.

• Slow-Motion Playback
Review your swing in slow motion at 0.25x, 0.5x, or full speed. Use frame-by-frame stepping to analyze key positions.

• Offline Support
Record and queue videos even without internet. Your swings will sync automatically when you're back online.

WHAT CAUSES A SLICE?

A slice typically results from one or more of these issues:
- Open clubface at impact
- Out-to-in (over-the-top) swing path
- Weak grip
- Poor body rotation
- Incorrect ball position

SliceFix AI helps you identify YOUR specific issue so you can focus your practice effectively.

SUBSCRIPTION OPTIONS

SliceFix AI offers a 7-day free trial with our annual plan. After the trial, choose the plan that works for you:

• Monthly: $8.99/month
• Annual: $59.99/year (Save 44%)

Free users can analyze one swing to experience the app before subscribing.

Payment will be charged to your Apple ID account. Subscriptions automatically renew unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. Manage subscriptions in your Apple ID Settings.

DISCLAIMER

SliceFix AI provides educational golf coaching guidance only. Results vary based on individual practice and physical ability. This app does not provide professional golf instruction, medical advice, or physical therapy. Consult a qualified professional for personalized guidance.

Download SliceFix AI today and take the first step toward straighter shots!
```
