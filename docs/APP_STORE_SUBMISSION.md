## App Description

### Description (4000 characters max)
```
SliceFix AI is your pocket golf coach powered by advanced artificial intelligence. Stop slicing and start hitting straighter shots with personalized swing analysis and drill recommendations.

HOW IT WORKS
1. Record or upload a video of your golf swing (5-8 seconds)
2. Our AI analyzes your swing for slice-causing issues
3. Get your personalized diagnosis and recommended drill
4. Practice with our guided 10-swing challenges
5. Track your progress over time

WHAT WE ANALYZE
SliceFix AI focuses on the four most common slice causes:
• Open Clubface at Impact
• Out-to-In Swing Path
• Early Extension
• Poor Alignment/Setup

PERSONALIZED DRILLS
Each analysis includes a specific drill designed to fix YOUR slice cause:
• Step-by-step instructions
• Recommended repetitions
• Common mistakes to avoid
• Progress tracking

10-SWING CHALLENGE
Build better habits with our structured practice challenges:
• 10 checkpoints per session
• Track completion rate
• See improvement over time

SUBSCRIPTION OPTIONS
• 7-day free trial with annual subscription
• Monthly: $8.99/month
• Annual: $59.99/year (save 44%)

Premium features include:
• Unlimited swing analyses
• Full drill library access
• Progress tracking & history
• Video comparison tools
• Priority support

PRIVACY FIRST
• Videos are processed and automatically deleted
• Your data is never sold to third parties
• Secure cloud storage for your analysis history

Whether you're a weekend golfer frustrated with your slice or a dedicated player looking to shave strokes, SliceFix AI provides the focused, actionable feedback you need.

Download now and start your journey to straighter shots!

Questions? Contact us at support@cienrios.com
```
