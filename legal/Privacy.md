## 2. Information We Collect

### A. Information You Provide

**Account Information**
- Email address (if you create an account or sign in)
- Apple ID identifier (when using Sign in with Apple)

**User Content**
- Golf swing videos you choose to upload

**Support Communications**
- Messages sent to customer support

### B. Information Collected Automatically

- App usage data (screens viewed, feature usage)
- Device information (iOS version, app version)
- Subscription status (active, expired, trial)

### C. Information We Do NOT Collect

- No precise location data
- No contacts

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Analyze uploaded swing videos and generate coaching feedback
- Provide app functionality and features
- Manage subscriptions and entitlements
- Respond to support requests
- Improve app performance and reliability
- Comply with legal obligations

---

## 4. Video & Data Retention

- Swing videos are processed solely to generate feedback
- Uploaded videos are automatically deleted after processing or within a short retention window
- You may delete your data at any time by deleting your account

---

## 5. Account Deletion & Data Removal

If you created an account, you may delete it directly in the app:

**Settings → Delete Account**

When you delete your account:
- Your account information is permanently removed
- Any stored swing data and metadata are deleted
- Subscription records remain only as required for accounting and legal compliance

---

## 6. Subscriptions & Payments

- Payments are processed by Apple App Store or authorized third-party providers
- We do not store your credit card or payment details
- Subscription status is used solely to unlock app features
